University of Sri Jayawardenapura

Established in 1959 as Vidyodaya University, the institution was later restructured and
renamed in 1978 to reflect its location in Sri Jayewardenepura Kotte, the administrative
capital of Sri Lanka. It is renowned for its emphasis on social sciences, humanities, management,
and applied sciences.
